Short answer

For a custom automotive seat cover RFQ, send target vehicle models, launch market, expected quantity, material preference, color direction, packaging needs and whether you want sample kits or wholesale sets first.

Buyer FAQ

What information should be included in a seat cover RFQ?

Buyer type, vehicle models, country, target quantity, preferred material, color, packaging and sample timeline.

How do I choose between custom-fit and universal seat covers?

Custom-fit is better for premium visual finish and model-specific programs. Universal sets are better for fast-moving wholesale ranges.

How can distributors increase order value?

Use seat covers as the hero product, then bundle steering wheel covers, mats, cushions and seat protectors.

What assets are needed before paid traffic?

Real installation photos, material close-ups, factory photos, packaging images and certificates.
