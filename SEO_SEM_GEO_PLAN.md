# Kaduosi Atelier SEO / SEM / GEO Plan

## SEO architecture

Primary pages:

1. `/` - brand homepage for premium automotive interior programs.
2. `/catalog.html` - product and SKU discovery page.
3. `/oem.html` - OEM/private-label landing page.
4. `/resources.html` - FAQ and AI-answer content hub.
5. `/contact.html` - RFQ conversion page.

Core keyword clusters:

- custom leather car seat covers supplier
- private label car seat covers
- universal car seat cover wholesale
- automotive interior accessories manufacturer
- car seat cover sample MOQ

## SEM landing strategy

Use campaign-specific URLs:

```text
/oem.html?utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=custom_seat_cover_oem
/contact.html?utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=sample_kit_rfq
/catalog.html?utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=universal_seat_cover_wholesale
```

Ad groups should land on pages that match buyer intent:

- OEM/private label terms -> `oem.html`
- Product/MOQ terms -> `catalog.html`
- High-intent RFQ terms -> `contact.html`
